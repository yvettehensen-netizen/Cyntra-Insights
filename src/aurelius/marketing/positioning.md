# Aurelius — Strategic Intelligence at Scale

## Market Positioning

**Category**: AI-Powered Strategic Consulting Platform
**Target**: SME to Mid-Market Companies (€1M-€100M revenue)
**Positioning**: "McKinsey-grade strategic analysis in 24 hours, at 1/100th the cost"

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Traditional Consulting is Broken:
- **Too Expensive**: €50K-€500K for basic strategic analysis
- **Too Slow**: 6-12 weeks for deliverables
- **Too Generic**: Cookie-cutter frameworks, limited customization
- **Too Detached**: Consultants don't understand your business reality

### DIY Strategic Planning Fails:
- **Internal Blind Spots**: Can't see your own biases
- **Limited Frameworks**: Don't know what "good" looks like
- **No Accountability**: Easy to avoid hard truths
- **Execution Gap**: Strategy docs gather dust

---

## Our Solution

**Aurelius** = 7-13 Expert AI Nodes analyzing your business in parallel:

1. **Strategy Node** — SWOT, competitive positioning, growth strategy
2. **Finance Node** — Cashflow, profitability, capital efficiency
3. **Team Dynamics** — Culture, leadership, engagement
4. **Undercurrent** — Political dynamics, unspoken truths
5. **Operations** — Process efficiency, automation opportunities
6. **Competitive Intelligence** — Market positioning, differentiation
7. **90-Day Plan** — Concrete actions with owners and deadlines

**BRUTAL MODE** adds 6 hard-truth nodes:
- Hard Truth Injector (uncomfortable reality checks)
- Vital Signs Monitor (organizational health)
- Stress Test Simulator (5 worst-case scenarios)
- Accountability Enforcer (ownership analysis)
- Benchmark Analyzer (industry comparison)
- Truth Delta Calculator (rhetoric vs. reality gap)
