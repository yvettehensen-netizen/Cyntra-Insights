# Aurelius Engine™ — Launch Plan

## 🎯 Launch Objectives

**Primary Goal**: Validate product-market fit with 100 paid analyses in first 90 days
**Revenue Goal**: €50K MRR by Month 6
**Market Position**: "The Brutal Truth Platform for CEOs"

---

## Phase 1: Stealth Launch (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1-2: Beta Testing
- **Target**: 20 friendly CEOs/founders
- **Offer**: Free analysis in exchange for detailed feedback
- **Goal**: Validate brutal mode reception, refine output quality
- **Success Metric**: 4+ star rating, 80%+ would pay

### Week 3-4: Case Study Development
- **Target**: 5 detailed case studies from beta
- **Content**: Before/after, testimonials, specific ROI
- **Goal**: Social proof for launch
- **Deliverable**: 5 case study PDFs + video testimonials

---

## Phase 2: Public Launch (Weeks 5-8)

### Week 5: Product Hunt Launch
- **Pre-launch**:
  - Build email list (500 subscribers)
  - Prepare launch assets (video demo, screenshots)
  - Hunter identified + prepped
- **Launch Day**:
  - Target: #1 Product of the Day
  - Team coordination: upvotes, comments, responses

### Week 6-7: Content Blitz
- **LinkedIn**: CEO posts brutal truths + case studies (daily)
- **Medium**: 3 articles on strategic mistakes CEOs make

- **Goal**: 10K impressions/day, 100 inbound leads

### Week 8: Founder Outreach
- **Cold outreach**: 500 targeted CEOs (€1M-€50M revenue)
- **Message**: "I ran your company through Aurelius. Found 3 critical gaps. Want the full report?"
- **Conversion target**: 5% → 25 paid analyses

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Brutal Mode Too Harsh
- **Mitigation**: Offer "Balanced Mode" as default, Brutal as opt-in
- **Monitoring**: Track completion rates, sentiment analysis

### Risk 2: Low Conversion Rates
- **Mitigation**: Offer free Quick Scan, upsell to Full Analysis
- **Monitoring**: Weekly conversion funnel analysis

### Risk 3: Quality Perception
- **Mitigation**: Publish case studies, show ROI, money-back guarantee
- **Monitoring**: NPS tracking, testimonial collection

### Risk 4: Market Timing
- **Mitigation**: Focus on companies in crisis (raising, scaling, struggling)
- **Monitoring**: Pipeline by trigger event
